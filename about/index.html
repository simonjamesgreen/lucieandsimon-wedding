About us

Our Story

Two people, two cities, one adventure — and a very good dog-to-human ratio.

From the streets of south-east London to the hills above Holmfirth, ours is a story of two worlds colliding in the best possible way. We’ve explored cities together, got happily lost on the moors, and built a life that somehow involves two dogs, a lot of miles, and never enough time to do everything we want to do together.

On 26 June 2027, in the village where Lucie grew up, we’re making it official.

Lucie

The Bride

Lucie grew up in the Holme Valley — proper Yorkshire through and through. She knows which paths are worth the mud, where to find the best pie, and how to make anywhere feel like home.

Warm, sharp, and quietly hilarious, Lucie has the rare ability to make every room better the moment she walks in. She’s the reason we always have a plan and the reason the plan is usually abandoned for something better.

She also has impeccable taste in dogs, which is how we ended up with two of them.

Simon

The Groom

Simon is a south Londoner who traded the city commute for longer commutes to everywhere else. He closes deals across time zones, plans trips to countries he can barely pronounce, and maintains an irrational optimism about getting to the airport on time.

Somehow — despite the travel, the dogs, and the inexplicable number of half-finished books on his bedside table — he managed to find Lucie. That’s the luckiest thing that ever happened to him.

He agrees completely about the dogs.

How we got here
