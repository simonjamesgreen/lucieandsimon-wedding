The story

South-east London meets the Holme Valley. Two dogs were also involved.

We met [add your story here]. From there it moved at the kind of pace that makes people ask “wait, how long have you two actually known each other?”

Since then: cities explored, moors got lost on, two dogs acquired, and a truly surprising number of hours spent debating Yorkshire walking routes. Lucie usually wins. She’s always right about the routes.

When Simon proposed, the answer was never in doubt — though the dogs did try to get involved. They always do. On 26 June 2027, in the village where Lucie grew up, we’re making it official.

Lucie

The bride

Holme Valley born and raised — proper Yorkshire. Knows which paths are worth the mud, where to find the best pie, and how to make anywhere feel like home.

Warm, sharp, and quietly hilarious. The reason we always have a plan, and the reason the plan usually gets abandoned for something better.

Simon

The groom

South Londoner who traded the city commute for longer commutes to everywhere else. Plans trips to countries he can barely pronounce and remains irrationally optimistic about airport timing.

Finding Lucie is the luckiest thing that ever happened to him. He agrees completely about the dogs.
