The Day

From vows in Honley to dancing in Lindley — here’s how the day unfolds.

The Ceremony — St Mary’s Church, Honley
12:00
Ceremony begins
We say our vows at St Mary’s Church, Honley. The ceremony lasts approximately 45 minutes to an hour. Please aim to be seated by 11:45.
1:00
Photos at the church
We’ll take some photos outside the church while guests begin making their way to Manor House, Lindley.
The Reception — Manor House, Lindley
1:30
Guests welcomed
Manor House opens its doors. Head straight in and grab a drink — the bar is open. We’ll follow once we’ve wrapped up the church photos.
2:00
Drinks reception & tea ceremony
A relaxed hour of free-flowing drinks, photography, and our tea ceremony. Mingle, explore the grounds, and enjoy the afternoon.
3:30
Please take your seats
Guests are invited into the dining room for the wedding breakfast.
3:45
Mr & Mrs — announced!
We make our entrance as a married couple, followed by a four-course wedding breakfast. This is the moment.
6:00
Speeches
Raise a glass. Prepare to laugh, cry, or both.
6:30
Comfort break
A chance to freshen up and stretch your legs while we transform the room for the evening.
The Evening
7:30
Evening celebrations begin
Evening guests are welcomed and the DJ gets things going.
8:00
Cake cutting & first dance
The moment you’ve all been waiting for.
8:45
Band — first set
Live music to get everyone on the floor.
9:30
Evening food
Fuel for the rest of the evening.
10:15
Band — second set
The second wind. DJ takes over as the night winds down.
Midnight
Last orders
Last call at the bar.
12:30
Bar closes
1:00 am
Goodnight & safe travels
We say our goodbyes. Thank you for sharing this day with us.
