Our Venues

A village church and a grand manor house — both in the heart of West Yorkshire.

01

The Ceremony

St Mary’s
Church, Honley

12:00 noon

St Mary’s Church
Church Street
Honley, Holmfirth
HD9 6AH

Nestled in the village of Honley in the Holme Valley, St Mary’s is a classic English parish church with a beautiful interior and a setting that feels genuinely timeless. Lucie grew up near here — it felt like the only place to do this.

The ceremony begins at 12:00 noon. Please aim to be seated by 11:45. There is limited parking in the village; we’d encourage guests to walk from nearby or share rides where possible.

02

The Reception

Manor House
Lindley

From 1:30 pm

Manor House
Lidget Street
Lindley, Huddersfield
HD3 3JB

Manor House Lindley is a stunning venue set in a beautifully restored historic building on the edge of Huddersfield. With elegant dining rooms, a superb kitchen, and grounds that look particularly wonderful in late June, it’s the perfect setting for an afternoon and evening of celebration.

Guests are welcome from 1:30 pm. The bar will be open throughout the afternoon and evening. There is parking on site.
