Getting there

Travel
& Stay

Everything you need to get to West Yorkshire and wake up feeling human the next morning.

By Train

The nearest main station is Huddersfield, well served by direct trains from London King’s Cross (via Wakefield Westgate) and London St Pancras (via Leeds or Sheffield). Journey time from London is around 2 hours 15 minutes.

From Huddersfield station, it’s a short taxi ride to both Honley (for the ceremony) and Lindley (for the reception).

Tip: Book your train tickets in advance — Avanti and LNER often have good advance fares. Huddersfield is also easy to reach from Leeds, Manchester, and Sheffield.

By Car

West Yorkshire is well connected by the M1 and M62 motorways.

  • From London — approx. 3 hrs via M1 North, exit J38 or J39
  • From Manchester — approx. 45 mins via M62 East
  • From Leeds — approx. 20 mins via A62
  • From Sheffield — approx. 30 mins via A616 or M1/M62

Please note: If you plan to drink at the reception (we strongly encourage it), please arrange accommodation locally or book a taxi in advance rather than driving home.

Taxis

We recommend booking taxis in advance, particularly if you’re staying locally and need a ride between venues or back to your hotel at the end of the night.

  • Huddersfield Hackney — 01484 123 456
  • A2B Radio Cars — local firm, reliable
  • Uber — available in the Huddersfield area

Please update with your preferred local numbers closer to the day.

Where to Stay

There are good options in Huddersfield town centre (closest to Manor House Lindley) and in the Holme Valley around Honley and Holmfirth.

  • Cedar Court Hotel — Huddersfield, 3★, close to the M62
  • Huddersfield Hotel — town centre, easy taxi distance
  • Fleece Inn, Holmfirth — charming pub with rooms, very local
  • Airbnb & cottages — the Holme Valley has lovely self-catering options, great for groups

We’d suggest booking accommodation as soon as possible — June is a popular time in the area.

The Holme Valley is genuinely one of the most beautiful parts of England — if you can arrive on Friday and make a weekend of it, we’d highly recommend it.
