Getting there

Travel & stay

How to reach West Yorkshire, and where to wake up the morning after.

Train

Huddersfield is the station

Direct from London King’s Cross via Wakefield Westgate, or St Pancras via Leeds or Sheffield — around 2h 15m from London. Easy connections from Leeds, Manchester, and Sheffield.

From the station it’s a short taxi to both Honley and Lindley. Book train tickets in advance for the best fares.

Car

M1 and M62 country

  • London — about 3 hours via the M1
  • Manchester — about 45 minutes via the M62
  • Leeds — about 20 minutes via the A62
  • Sheffield — about 30 minutes via the A616

If you plan to drink — and we’d encourage it — stay locally or book a taxi rather than driving.

Taxi

Book before the day

Taxis between the church and Manor House, and back to your hotel at the end of the night, are best arranged in advance. Uber operates in the Huddersfield area; local firm numbers to follow with the invitations.

Stay

Huddersfield or the Holme Valley

  • Huddersfield town centre — closest to Manor House, easy taxis
  • Holmfirth & Honley — pubs with rooms and holiday cottages, close to the church
  • Cottages & Airbnb — the valley is full of good self-catering, ideal for groups

June is busy in the valley — book early.

Worth knowing

The Holme Valley is one of the best-looking corners of England. Come Friday, make a weekend of it.
