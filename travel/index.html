By rail

Huddersfield is the station. Direct trains run from London King’s Cross via Wakefield Westgate, and from St Pancras via Leeds or Sheffield. From the station it’s a ten-minute taxi to either venue.

Advance tickets are dramatically cheaper — book as soon as your plans firm up.

By car

The valley sits just off the M62 — junction 24 for Lindley. Manor House has parking on site, and cars can stay overnight if you’re taxiing home. Parking in Honley village is limited, so share lifts to the church where you can.

If you plan to drink — and we’d encourage it — please don’t drive.

Getting home

Uber works well here. Simon works for Uber and has assured us there’s plenty of supply — including at 1am. To lock in your ride, Uber Reserve lets you pre-book in the app.

Open Uber — pickup at Manor House

Prefer a local firm? Book your end-of-night ride in advance — 1am on a Saturday is peak demand.
