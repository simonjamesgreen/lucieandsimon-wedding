Getting there

Travel

Both venues sit just outside Huddersfield, West Yorkshire — easy by road or rail.

The venues

Two stops, twenty minutes apart

St Mary’s Church — Church Street, Honley, Holmfirth HD9 6AH · Directions

Manor House — Lidget Street, Lindley, Huddersfield HD3 3JB · Directions

The drive between the two is around 20 minutes. Please arrange your lift or taxi before the day.

By train

Huddersfield is the station

Direct from London King’s Cross via Wakefield Westgate, or St Pancras via Leeds or Sheffield — about 2 hours 15 minutes from London. Quick connections from Leeds, Manchester, and Sheffield.

From the station it’s a short taxi to either venue. Advance tickets are much cheaper — book early.

By car

Just off the M62

  • London — around 3 hours via the M1
  • Manchester — around 45 minutes via the M62
  • Leeds — around 20 minutes via the A62
  • Sheffield — around 30 minutes via the A616

Manor House has parking on site. Parking in Honley village is limited — please share cars where possible.

Taxis

Book before the day

Taxis between the church and Manor House, and home at the end of the night, are best arranged in advance — especially for the 1am finish. Uber operates in the Huddersfield area; local firm recommendations will follow with the invitations.

If you plan to drink — we’d encourage it — please don’t drive.
