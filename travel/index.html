Getting there

Travel

Two venues, twenty minutes apart, just outside Huddersfield, West Yorkshire.

The Ceremony · 12 noon

St Mary’s Church, Honley

Church Street, Honley, Holmfirth HD9 6AH

Open in Google Maps Open in Apple Maps

Please be seated by 11:45. Parking in the village is limited — car-share where you can, or take a taxi.

The Reception · from 1:30pm

Manor House, Lindley

Lidget Street, Lindley, Huddersfield HD3 3JB

Open in Google Maps Open in Apple Maps

Parking on site. The bar is open from 1:30pm until half past midnight.

By train

Huddersfield is the station

Direct from London King’s Cross via Wakefield Westgate, or St Pancras via Leeds or Sheffield — about 2 hours 15 minutes from London. Quick connections from Leeds, Manchester, and Sheffield. From the station it’s a short taxi to either venue.

By car

Just off the M62

  • London — around 3 hours via the M1
  • Manchester — around 45 minutes via the M62
  • Leeds — around 20 minutes via the A62
  • Sheffield — around 30 minutes via the A616
Taxis

Uber works well here

Simon works for Uber and has assured us there’s plenty of supply in the area — including at the end of the night. If you’d rather lock in your ride ahead of time, Uber Reserve lets you pre-book a car in the app.

Open Uber — pickup at Manor House

Prefer a local firm? These are well-rated and all a short hop from Lindley:

  • Mount Taxis (Marsh) — 01484 515006 · 4.6★ on Google
  • Woods Taxis (Salendine Nook) — 01484 400400 · 4.6★ on Google
  • Huddersfield Taxis (24hr, app available) — 01484 300030 · 4.3★ on Google

Whichever you choose, book your end-of-night ride before the day — 1am on a Saturday is peak demand. And if you plan to drink (we’d encourage it), please don’t drive.
