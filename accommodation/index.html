Where to stay

Accommodation

From boutique rooms at the venue itself to cottages in the Holme Valley.

At the venue

Manor House Lindley

The venue itself has eleven individually designed boutique bedrooms — original Georgian sash windows, exposed stone, and you’re already home when the bar closes. These will go quickly, so if you’d like one, contact the hotel as soon as possible and mention the wedding.

manorhouselindley.co.uk

Nearby hotels

Huddersfield & around

  • Huddersfield town centre — a short taxi from Manor House, with a range of hotels at sensible prices
  • Cedar Court Hotel — just off the M62, ten minutes from Lindley by car
  • The Old Golf House Hotel, Outlane — close to Lindley, quieter setting
The valley

Honley & Holmfirth

If you’d rather wake up in the countryside, the Holme Valley around Honley and Holmfirth has lovely pubs with rooms and plenty of holiday cottages — ideal if you fancy making a weekend of it. You’d be close to the church, with a 20-minute taxi back from Manor House at night.

  • Cottages & Airbnb — great for families and groups; June books up early
  • Pubs with rooms — characterful and very local
Our advice

Book early, stay two nights

Late June is peak season in the valley. If you can, arrive on the Friday — the area is genuinely one of the most beautiful corners of England, and you won’t want to rush off on the Sunday either.
