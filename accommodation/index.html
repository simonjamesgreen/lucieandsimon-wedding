Where to stay

Accommodation

Hand-picked places within easy reach of Manor House — from a two-minute stroll to countryside inns. Google ratings and guide prices included; book direct for the best rates.

I.

Closest to the Venue

Walk home from the reception — no taxi required.

2 min walk · Lindley

Plover Cottage

★★★★★ 4.8 (139 reviews)

Beautifully kept cottages moments from Manor House — guests at previous weddings there rave about it. Spacious, spotless, with parking. The single best-located option there is.

£90–130 / night, guide

Book Map

10 min walk · Lindley

Heritage Serviced Suites

★★★★½ 4.4 (76 reviews)

Apartment-style suites with kitchens and living space — previous Manor House wedding guests recommend them. On-site parking, gym, and a famously good café breakfast next door.

£80–120 / night, guide

Book Map

1 mile · Birchencliffe

Briar Court Hotel

★★★★ 4.3 (450 reviews)

A friendly, well-run hotel five minutes' drive away, with a well-regarded Italian restaurant on site. Comfortable rooms and warm Yorkshire service.

£80–110 / night, guide

Book Map
II.

Best on a Budget

Clean, reliable, and kind to the wallet.

1.5 miles · Ainley Top

Fortune Hotel (Best Western)

★★★★½ 4.4 (392 reviews)

The area’s hidden bargain — spacious, spotless rooms from around £45, free parking, and a proper old pub with an open fire next door. Guests are consistently amazed at the value.

£45–75 / night, guide

Book Map

3 miles · Town centre

Premier Inn Huddersfield Central

★★★★ 4.2 (906 reviews)

Exactly what you expect from a Premier Inn — comfy beds, soundproofed windows, canal views, and a team guests genuinely rave about. Short taxi to both venues.

£60–90 / night, guide

Book Map

1.5 miles · Ainley Top

Cedar Court Hotel

★★★★ 3.8 (1,895 reviews)

A large hotel right by the M62 with recently refurbished public areas, a bar, and dog-friendly rooms — handy for bigger groups who want to stay together.

£85–120 / night, guide

III.

Family-Friendly

Space to spread out, kitchens, and room for the kids.

10 min walk · Lindley

Heritage Serviced Suites

★★★★½ 4.4 (76 reviews)

The apartments here are ideal for families — separate living areas, full kitchens for early breakfasts and bedtime routines, and you can walk to the reception.

£80–120 / night, guide

2 min walk · Lindley

Plover Cottage (cottages)

★★★★★ 4.8 (139 reviews)

Adjoining cottage apartments that sleep four comfortably — perfect for a family or two couples sharing, with parking and two bathrooms in some units.

£90–130 / night, guide

3 miles · Town centre

Premier Inn (family rooms)

★★★★ 4.2 (906 reviews)

Family rooms sleeping up to four at sensible prices, an on-site restaurant for easy dinners, and staff known for being brilliant with kids.

IV.

Country Inns & Character

A little further out, but worth every mile — and handy for the church.

7 miles · Thunder Bridge

The Woodman Inn

★★★★½ 4.5 (1,256 reviews)

A gorgeous stone inn in a wooded hamlet — beautiful rooms (some with huge baths), superb food, and one of our favourite spots in the area. Genuinely special.

£110–150 / night, guide

Book Map

6 miles · Holmfirth

The Huntsman Inn

★★★★½ 4.5 (2,045 reviews)

Set in the hills above Holmfirth with knockout views, lovely rooms, and a Sunday roast people drive across the county for. Close to the church side of the day.

£100–140 / night, guide

Book Map

5 miles · Holmfirth

Rooms at the Nook

★★★★½ 4.4 (42 reviews)

Cosy rooms above a brilliant little brewery pub in the middle of Holmfirth — full English included, great pubs on the doorstep, and proper Last of the Summer Wine country.

£70–90 / night, guide
